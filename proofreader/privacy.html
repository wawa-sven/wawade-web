Privacy Policy for Proofreader

Effective date: October 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Wawade AB ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy when you use our Google Docs add-on, Proofreader (the "Add-On"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights in relation to that information.

By installing or using the Add-On, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. What information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • When you install and grant permissions to the Add-On, you may provide us with your Google account identifier (for example, via OAuth) only to the extent necessary for the Add-On’s functionality.
  • If you contact us (for example, for support) you may supply your email address and related information.

2.2 Information automatically collected or processed

  • The Add-On accesses the content of your Google Docs document only insofar as needed to perform proofreading tasks, such as reading the text of the document.
  • Where necessary to generate proofreading suggestions, the Add-On may transmit relevant text to third-party AI services for the duration of the request.
  • In some cases, small excerpts of document text may be stored temporarily to diagnose issues or improve the service. These excerpts are used only for diagnostics and are not used for any other purpose, including training AI models.
  • We do not collect or store personal identifiers (such as your name or address), unless you explicitly submit such data, for example in a support request.
  • We may log non-content usage data (for example, feature usage or error codes) to help maintain and improve the Add-On.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain the Add-On’s functionality.
  • To monitor usage and improve the Add-On, such as performance monitoring, fixing bugs, or developing new features.
  • To respond to your support inquiries or requests.
  • We do not use your document content for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing purposes, and we do not sell or lease your data.

4. Data sharing and disclosure

  • We will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share usage statistics with trusted third-party services, such as analytics providers.
  • If we transmit document text to third-party AI services, it is only to perform the requested proofreading task. The service provider does not retain the content or use it to train models.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or the safety of users.

5. Data retention

  • Document content is processed in real time and is not stored by us permanently.
  • If diagnostic logs are generated, they may include excerpts of document text. These logs are stored for up to 30 days and then permanently deleted.

6. Security

  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
  • Data transmissions, where relevant, are encrypted (for example, via HTTPS).
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. International transfers

If document text or diagnostic logs are processed by third-party AI services located outside your jurisdiction (for example, outside the EU/EEA), we will apply appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable law.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (especially if you are in the EU under the General Data Protection Regulation, GDPR), you may have the following rights:

  • Access: You can ask what personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You may ask us to correct incomplete or inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data (including any diagnostic logs).
  • Restriction/Objection: You may ask us to restrict processing or object to processing, including diagnostic logging.
  • Data portability: If applicable, you may request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@wawade.com.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will indicate when it was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. If we make material changes, we will notify you via the Add-On interface or our website.
